# 🧠 My Second Brain

> A minimalist, organic, and friction-free space for capturing thoughts, tracking projects, and connecting ideas.

This repository is structured as a premium **Obsidian Vault**. It is designed to get out of your way and let you write, link, and learn.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Download & Install**: Download [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) if you haven't already.
2. **Open Vault**: Open Obsidian, click **"Open folder as vault"**, and choose this folder (`brain/`).
3. **Start Reading**: Double-click **`Welcome.md`** at the root of the vault for an interactive guided tour and a quick 3-minute setup!

---

## 📂 Vault Structure

* **`00 Inbox/`** — Frictionless landing pad for quick captures.
* **`10 Daily/`** — Journaling and daily bullet logs.
* **`20 Brain/`** — Permanent evergreen notes and book/media summaries.
* **`30 Projects/`** — Action plans and tasks for active projects.
* **`40 Templates/`** — Markdown blueprints for consistent note structures.
* **`50 Archive/`** — Storage space for completed or inactive files.

---

## 🧠 Philosophy: Frictionless Capture, Organic Connection

1. **Inbox First**: Capture raw ideas instantly without worrying about where they go. Sort them later.
2. **Atomic Writing**: Write short, focused notes about a single concept so they can be easily linked.
3. **Links Over Folders**: Use double brackets `[[Note Name]]` to tie ideas together. Let your folder structures remain flat and let connections grow organically.

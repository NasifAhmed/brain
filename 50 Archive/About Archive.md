# 📦 About the Archive

Welcome to the **Archive**!

This is the storage room of your Second Brain. 

### 💡 Why archive instead of delete?
Storage is cheap, but context is priceless. If a project ends, or a resource is no longer active, **do not delete it.**
- Move it to `50 Archive/`.
- This keeps your active workspace (`10 Daily`, `20 Brain`, `30 Projects`) completely clean and high-focus.
- But if you ever need to recall *how* you solved a problem three years ago, or what you researched, the search bar (`Ctrl + N` or `Ctrl + F`) and the backlinks will find it instantly in the Archive!

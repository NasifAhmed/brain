# 📆 About Daily Logs

Welcome to your **Daily Logs** folder!

This folder keeps a chronological history of your days. Keeping these notes in a dedicated folder ensures they don't clutter your evergreen ideas in `20 Brain`.

### 💡 How to use Daily Notes
A daily note is a low-pressure diary of your actions, observations, and tasks.
- Use the **`Daily Note`** template in `40 Templates/` to quickly format your daily log.
- Track a single core focus for the day.
- Log thoughts or links to other notes you worked on today (e.g. `Worked on [[Project - Design My Second Brain]] today`).
- Write reflections to clear your head at the end of the day.

### 🔗 Connecting Today's Log
Obsidian's magic comes from links. When you mention a concept or a project in your daily log, use double brackets `[[Project Name]]`. This builds a powerful historical trace so when you open a project, you can look at the "Backlinks" panel and see exactly which days you worked on it!
